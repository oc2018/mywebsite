about

Eric mutugi Ndege

I was born in the east side of the slopes of mount kenya, in a small town known as Nkubu, Meru county. I joined Gankondi primary school and Kanyakine Boys Boarding for my primary education before proceeding to Meru School for my secondary education. I then joined Jomo Kenyatta University but dropped out due to finacial constrains.
I then joined Kenya power as a meter reader and KASNEB for an ICT course that i completed in January 2018. I hava always had passion for programming so I decided to take up coding as a hobby. I first started with Java servlets in tomcat server and Mysql database that I had used for my ICT project but it was not flexible enough. I learnt about JavaScript and decided to try it out.
JavaScript was flexible - ReactJs/Next, Material UI, Redux front-end formed beautiful client side while NodeJS, expressjs back-end offered the flexibility i was looking for. MongoDB provided a clound database that was ideal for web apps as it does not require installation.
